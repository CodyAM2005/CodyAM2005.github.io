Problem statement: Captcha difficulty

The struggle users' face when doing captchas. We believe the process should be more engaging and easier to understand.

Affinity diagram: Lollipop Lickers Affinity Diagram

Our team, the Lollipop Lickers, took notes to brainstorm some ideas for a way to improve captchas. We compiled all of these into an affinity diagram, with the titles, "Base Ideas", "Captcha Problems", "What is Captcha doing right?", "Restrictions", and "Potential Solutions".

Team Personas: Lollipop Lickers Team Personas

My persona's person is Mark Jones. He says "Fortnite is important but Fortnite is importanter," and he is a developer for Epic Games. He dislikes Captcha's, because his career requires logging into multiple accounts, which becomes tedious.

Team Storyboard: Lollipop Lickers Team Storyboard

My storyboard has a main character, which was my persona Mark Jones. He gets to work, but because of all the accounts he handles, he has to do CAPTCHAs all day which takes away from his productivity. He wants a faster and easier CAPTCHA experience.
